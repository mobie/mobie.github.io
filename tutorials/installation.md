## Installation of MoBIE

1. [Install Fiji](https://fiji.sc) on your computer.  
2. Open Fiji and go to `Help > Update...` in the menu.  
3. Click `[ Manage Update Sites ]`.  
4. Scroll down to click the checkbox next to `MoBIE`.
6. Click `[ Apply and Close ]`.  
7. Restart Fiji.
8. Find all the MoBIE functionality in `Plugins > MoBIE > ...`

To confirm that everything works you may open a published MoBIE project via:
`Plugins > MoBIE > Open > Project > Open Published MoBIE Project`
and choose any of the drop down options.
